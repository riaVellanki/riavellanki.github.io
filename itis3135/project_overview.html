Project Overview

  • The UNC Charlotte Tamil student organization's website serves as a dynamic platform for promoting the club's activities and fostering community engagement. Through regular updates, event listings, and photo galleries, the website keeps members informed about upcoming gatherings, cultural celebrations, and opportunities for involvement. Additionally, features such as blog posts, resources, and forums facilitate ongoing communication and collaboration among members, ensuring a vibrant and connected Tamil community on campus.
  • Intended Users:
    1. Club Members
    2. Prospective Club Members
    3. General Public
  • Website Overview
    1. Home: Gives basic info about club
    2. About Us: Gives in depth information about club and it's history/background
    3. Contact Us: Provides links to social media and other forms of contact like email and a contact form
    4. Upcoming Events: Gives information on upcoming events and meetings
    5. Past Events: Shows prospective members and general public what kind of things we do in the club
